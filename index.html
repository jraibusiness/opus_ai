Why Opus AI is different

Technology with purpose

We don't install tools. We build systems that solve your specific problem.

Measurable results

Every project has a clear success metric. Time saved. Costs reduced. Process eliminated.

A partner, not a vendor

We talk business without jargon. You understand what's being built and why.

Opus AI was founded by João Rocha — conductor, composer and AI automation specialist. The whole journey took shape from a personal need: as an artist, reconciling the time and energy spent on administrative tasks — especially those demanding constant follow-up — with the time devoted to conducting and composition. Solving my own operational bottlenecks became the method I now apply to institutions with the profile we serve. We understand this world deeply because we live inside it.
